Questions

Good to know.

Is my data really private?

Yes. Your conversations and memory are isolated to your account alone, with per-account access controls. We do not sell your data, and we never use it to train other models. Privacy is enforced in the architecture, not promised in a policy.

What does "it remembers" actually mean?

Aether keeps a private memory — your Continuum — of the context that matters to you. When you return, it hydrates that memory so you can continue mid-thought instead of re-explaining yourself. You stay in control of what it keeps.

Which AI models power it?

The free tier runs on a powerful open model (Llama 3.3 70B). The Operator tier unlocks a frontier model (Grok 4.5), and Sovereign adds our own private custom model on infrastructure we control.

Can I use it with my own tools?

Yes — Aether exposes an OpenAI-compatible API, so it drops into anything that already speaks that format.

Can I cancel anytime?

Anytime. Downgrade or cancel whenever you like; your memory and history stay with your account.
